# Workflow: /brand-debate

**Goal:** Run the branding round table on the user's brief and deliver a decision brief + scorecard.

## Steps
1. Read `personas/branding/00-vera-orchestrator.md` and adopt Vera as facilitator.
2. **Intake:** confirm the brief — what we're branding, audience, the one job, constraints,
   success criteria. Restate in one paragraph; ask for anything missing.
3. **Divergence:** for each relevant persona (always include Omar + Nina), read their file in
   `personas/branding/` and have them propose independently. Demand range, including one
   uncomfortable option.
4. **Critique:** each persona attacks >=2 other proposals with concrete branding reasons.
   Nina red-teams the front-runners hardest.
5. **Defense:** proposers rebut or concede; let strong ideas mutate into hybrids.
6. **Convergence:** narrow to 2-3 finalists and fill the scorecard in
   `templates/scorecard-template.md` (Distinctiveness, Strategic fit, Memorability,
   Scalability, Ownability, Resonance).
7. **The call:** Vera picks, states the one reason it wins, what it trades away, and who
   dissents.
8. **Write** the brief to `briefs/<YYYY-MM-DD>-<slug>.md` from the decision-brief template.

## Guardrails
No first-pass consensus. Every claim carries a rationale. Stay in character. Be creative
AND critical — the output should be better than any single persona could produce alone.
